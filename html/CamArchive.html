Nishika n8000

The most popular of all the stereo-cameras, Nishika stands out for being the biggest and probably most inconviniently designed but with the best results. The unique look of this camera makes it distinct from not just other film cameras, but also from other stereo-cameras. This camera has a light meter and is powered by 2AA batteries.
